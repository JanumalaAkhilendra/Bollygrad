Admission Process

+

Follow these steps to apply for admission:

  1. Check the eligibility criteria for your desired program.
  2. Fill out the online application form or download the form from our website.
  3. Submit the required documents (transcripts, ID proof, etc.).
  4. Pay the application fee online or via bank transfer.
  5. Attend the entrance exam or interview (if applicable).
  6. Wait for the admission decision and follow further instructions.

Important Dates:

  • Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
  • Entrance Exam: April 15, 2024
  • Admission Results: May 1, 2024

How to Apply:

  1. Submit the scholarship application form along with your admission application.
